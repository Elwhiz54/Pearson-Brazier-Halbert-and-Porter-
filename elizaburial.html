The twenty first of July, 1900, we, the undersigned priests, have buried in the cemetery of this parish the body of Ezilda LaRoche who died July 19th at the age of 41, wife of Henry W Pearson, of Sainte Marguerite of Dorchester. Those present at time of burial were: Philias Marchand, Albert Pearson, Edmund Pearson, Georges Pearson, William H Pearson who have signed with us. Reading done. (Side note of record : + Mass. E.U. = Massachusetts United States)
